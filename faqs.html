Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about how we operate.

Most agencies train agents to read. Our VAs not only utilise evidence-based scripts but are also trained to connect with clients. We prioritise conversation quality over Dial Count. Our internal QA team monitors calls to ensure agents are having real human interactions, not just hitting quotas.

Before they dial, they review the property’s aerial footage to identify distress (roof, yard, neglect). This isn’t telemarketing; it’s a verified inquiry.

Our agents are tested on their ability to structure and categorise a lead, distinguishing between a "Cash Offer" (Urgent) and a "Retail Listing" (Patient) within the first 2 minutes. If they can't structure a deal, they don't get on the phone.

We track everything using our Daily Performance KPIs:

  • Pipeline Velocity: How fast are leads moving from "new" to "contract"?
  • Contact & Pick-Up Rates: Are we calling at the right times?
  • Talk Time vs. Dial Time: Are VAs having conversations or just hearing dial tones?
  • Call-to-Appointment Ratio: How many conversations turn into scheduled appointments?
  • Lead Quality: We QC (Quality Control) every appointment to ensure it meets your buying criteria.

You can bring your own data, or we can build it for you. Unlike dialers who burn through recycled lists, VoloLeads builds custom Asset Lists using Virtual Drivers. We visually verify the distress of every property before it enters the dialer. This lowers your spam risk and raises your conversion rate because we have a reason to call.

We use our concession method. Our agents are trained never to argue. When a seller yells, our agents agree ("You're right, spammers are annoying") to lower the seller's guard, then pivot back to the evidence ("I only called because I saw the roof damage"). We turn "No" into a conversation, or we disqualify them immediately so you don't waste time on retail buyers.
